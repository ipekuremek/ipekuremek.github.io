What is Yoga?

Yoga: 'yoke' or 'union', is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India and aim to control (yoke) and still the mind, recognizing a detached witness-consciousness untouched by the mind (Chitta) and mundane suffering (Duḥkha).

A power yoga sequence

Power yoga, as you may understand from it's name, is not a very traditional type of a yoga. More often it's kind of an active yoga type. Planks and lunges can be longer to hold and some lunge pulses, core activation cruches can be seen in this yoga type. It's really creates some fire inside of you. That's what I love about power yoga. Without forcing myself I can feel my muscles and my breathes in the movement.
